Rental Policies

These policies help protect your booking and our inventory.

  • A deposit is required to reserve your event date.
  • Remaining balance is due before delivery or pickup.
  • Clients are responsible for damaged, missing, or lost rental items.
  • Delivery and pickup windows will be confirmed before the event.
  • Rental items must be returned in the same condition received.
  • Outdoor events should have a weather backup plan.
  • Cancellations may be subject to deposit forfeiture depending on timing.
